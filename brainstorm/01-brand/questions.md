# Brand Identity - Questions to Explore

## About the Name

- What feeling should the name evoke?
- Should it hint at AI/tech or stay neutral?
- One word or two? Abstract or descriptive?
- Any words you love? Any you hate?
- Names of agencies you admire?

## About the Positioning

- Who is NOT your client?
- What makes you different from other agencies?
- What's the one thing you want to be known for?
- If a client described you to a friend, what would they say?

## About Values

- What do you care about in how you work?
- What would you never compromise on?
- How do you want clients to feel working with you?

## About Voice

- Formal or casual?
- Technical or accessible?
- Bold or understated?

## Visual Direction

- Colors that resonate with you?
- Styles you're drawn to? (minimal, bold, playful, serious)
- Any brands whose look you admire?
