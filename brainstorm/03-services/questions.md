# Services - Questions to Explore

## What to Offer

- What types of projects excite you?
- What are you best at?
- What do clients actually need most?
- What can AI handle well vs needs human touch?

## What NOT to Offer

- What projects would you turn down?
- What's outside your expertise?
- What's not worth the hassle?

## Service Definition

- How do you describe what you do in one sentence?
- What's included in a "standard" project?
- What's always extra?

## Delivery

- Realistic turnaround times?
- What's your AI speed advantage?
- How do you handle revisions?

## Quality

- What does "done" look like?
- What's your quality bar?
- How do you test/QA?
