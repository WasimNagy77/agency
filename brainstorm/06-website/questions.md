# Agency Website - Questions to Explore

## Purpose

- What's the #1 goal of the site?
- What action should visitors take?
- Who is the primary audience?

## Content

- What pages do you need?
- What's the core message?
- How do you explain AI-powered without scaring people?

## Portfolio

- How do you show work when starting fresh?
- Case studies or project galleries?
- Show the process or just results?

## Trust Building

- How do you establish credibility?
- Testimonials strategy?
- Show UK registration?

## Technical

- What tech stack?
- Self-hosted or platform?
- How important is speed/performance?

## Domain

- Ideas for domain name?
- .com, .co.uk, or something else?
- Backup options if first choice taken?
