# UK Legal Setup - Questions to Explore

## Company Structure

- Ltd vs LLP - what matters more to you? (simplicity, liability, tax)
- Just you, or planning to bring in partners?
- Do you need to invoice internationally?

## Registration

- Company name - same as brand or different trading name?
- Registered address - home or virtual office?
- SIC codes - what business activities to register?

## Banking

- Which banks are you considering?
- Do you need multi-currency?
- Integration with accounting software?

## Accounting

- DIY vs accountant?
- VAT registration - now or later?
- What accounting software?

## Contracts & Legal

- What contracts do you need? (client agreement, NDA, etc.)
- Insurance - professional indemnity?
- GDPR compliance for client data?
