# Pricing - Questions to Explore

## Philosophy

- Compete on price or value?
- Fixed price or hourly?
- Transparent pricing or custom quotes?

## Market Position

- Premium, mid-market, or budget?
- Who are you competing with?
- What do they charge?

## Structure

- Tiers/packages or fully custom?
- What's in each tier?
- What triggers upgrades?

## Numbers

- Minimum project you'd take?
- Sweet spot project size?
- Maximum before it's too complex?

## Terms

- Payment schedule? (upfront, milestones, on delivery)
- What if scope changes?
- Refund policy?

## Ongoing

- Maintenance/retainer packages?
- Hosting fees?
- Support hours?
