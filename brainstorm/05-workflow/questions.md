# Client Workflow - Questions to Explore

## Finding You

- How do clients discover you?
- What's the first touchpoint?
- What makes them reach out?

## Inquiry to Proposal

- What info do you need from them?
- How do you qualify leads?
- How fast do you respond?
- What's in a proposal?

## Onboarding

- What do you need to start?
- How do you kick off a project?
- What tools/platforms do you use?

## During the Project

- How often do you communicate?
- How do you share progress?
- How do clients give feedback?
- What decisions need client input?

## The 13 Agents

- How do the agents collaborate?
- What's the handoff between agents?
- What does the orchestrator do?
- What needs human oversight?

## Delivery

- What does handoff look like?
- What documentation do you provide?
- How do you train clients?

## After Delivery

- Support period?
- How do you handle bugs?
- Upsell to maintenance?
