# Growth Plan - Questions to Explore

## First Clients

- Where will your first 10 clients come from?
- Existing network?
- What's your outreach strategy?

## Target Client

- Who's your ideal client?
- What industries?
- What size businesses?
- What problems do they have?

## Marketing

- What channels make sense?
- Content marketing?
- Paid ads?
- Referrals?

## Scaling

- At what point is it "working"?
- When do you need help?
- What would you hire for first?

## Revenue Goals

- Year 1 target?
- What's "success" look like?
- What's the minimum to sustain?

## Risks

- What could go wrong?
- What's your backup plan?
- What assumptions are you making?
